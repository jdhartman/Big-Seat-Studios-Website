Big Seat Studios was founded in December of 2015. Since then, we have been a development team of two. We have started our company with the passion to make games that we would play. Our goal is to create engaging experiences for our fans and a sense of personality within our games. While we are not a big, fancy game studio (our name can be a little misleading), we know what we like in games and we put our heart and soul into the products we make.
